PROSPECT (4, 5, or 5B) model

Usage

prospect(param, version, include.wl = FALSE)

Arguments

param
Vector of PROSPECT parameter values: N: Effective number of leaf layers (>1) Cab: Leaf chlorophyll content (ug/cm2) (>0) (5) Car: Leaf carotenoid content (ug/cm2) (>0) (5B) Cbrown: Leaf brown matter content (ug/cm2) (>0) Cw: Leaf water content (cm) (>0) Cm: Leaf dry matter content (ug/cm2) (>0)
version
PROSPECT version: 4, 5, or "5B"
include.wl
Whether or not to append wavelengths to output matrix. Included to provide some backward compatibility and benchmarking, but will soon be deprecated.

Value

Matrix (2101 x 3) of simulated reflectance (column 1, "R"), transmittance (column 2, "T"), and wavelength (column 3, "wl") values from 400:2500 nm

Details

R wrapper for PROSPECT models
