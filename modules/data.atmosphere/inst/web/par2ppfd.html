par2ppfd

Usage

par2ppfd(watts)

Arguments

PAR
(W / m2)

Value

PPFD (mol / m2 / s)

Description

convert PAR to PPFD

Details

Converts photosynthetically active radiation (PAR, units of Watts / m2) to photosynthetic photon flux density (PPFD) in units of mol / m2 / s From Campbell and Norman p151 PPFD = PAR * (J/m2/s) * (1 mol / 2.35e5 J) 2.35e5 J / mol is the energy content of solar radiation in the PAR waveband 0.486 is based on the approximation that PAR is 0.45-0.50 of the total radiation
